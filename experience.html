Experience



Industrial Training at PPANS

From February to March 2021, I had my industrial training at Perbadanan Perpustakaan Awam. I spent 6 weeks in experiencing how a librarian works. Overall, the whole experience was pretty educational and fun. Also, I befriended many trainees at the library and they are very kind and sweet to me. Not only that, I had also experienced how to handle an event or activities to public. For example, I conducted 2 activities with children that visited the library. In conclusion, I think industrial training is very good for students as it introduce them to work life and system, so that we would not be shocked when working in the future.
